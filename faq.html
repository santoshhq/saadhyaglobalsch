Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Saadhya Global School

Admission Related

The admission process includes filling out an online application form, submitting required documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, photographs), attending an interaction session with the child and parents, and completing the registration formalities. Visit our Admission page for detailed information and to download the application form.

We admit students from Nursery to Class 12. For Nursery admission, the child should be 3 years old as of March 31st of the academic year. Age criteria for other classes follow CBSE guidelines.

Admissions typically open in November-December for the next academic year starting in April. However, we accept applications throughout the year based on seat availability. Please contact our admission office for current availability.

Academic Programs

Saadhya Global School follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which is recognized nationally and internationally. Our curriculum emphasizes conceptual learning, critical thinking, and holistic development.

We maintain an optimal teacher-student ratio of 1:25 to ensure personalized attention for each child. For primary classes, we have smaller class sizes to provide more individualized support.

Yes, we offer a variety of after-school programs including sports coaching, music and dance classes, art workshops, robotics club, language classes, and homework support. These programs run on weekdays after regular school hours.

Facilities & Infrastructure

Yes, we provide safe and reliable bus transportation covering various routes across Hyderabad. All our buses are GPS-tracked and equipped with CCTV cameras. Each bus has a trained driver and a female attendant for student safety.

Student safety is our top priority. We have 24/7 CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, controlled entry/exit points, fire safety equipment, regular safety drills, medical room with trained staff, and comprehensive child protection policies. Visit our Safe Environment page for detailed information.

Yes, we have a well-stocked library with over 5,000 books, digital resources, and quiet reading spaces. Our modern computer lab is equipped with the latest computers, high-speed internet, and educational software. Students have regular access to both facilities as part of their curriculum.

General Information

School timings are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM for all classes. The school operates Monday through Saturday. Some Saturdays may be designated as activity days with modified timings. The office is open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on school days.

We maintain regular communication with parents through our parent portal, monthly newsletters, progress reports, parent-teacher meetings (PTMs), and SMS/email updates. Our online portal provides real-time access to attendance, academic performance, assignments, and school announcements.

Our fee structure varies by grade level and includes tuition fees, activity fees, and other charges. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Payment can be made quarterly, semi-annually, or annually. Please contact our admission office at 08008344244 or email info@saadhyaglobal.com for detailed fee information and available payment plans.
